About HavenBlink

Founded in 2015, HavenBlink has established itself as a premier home décor consultancy firm in the United States. Our team of experienced designers and decorators are passionate about creating beautiful, functional spaces that tell your unique story.

We believe that your living space should be a reflection of your personality, lifestyle, and aspirations. Our approach combines timeless design principles with contemporary trends to deliver spaces that are both beautiful and practical.

With over 300 successful projects completed across residential and commercial spaces, we bring expertise, creativity, and attention to detail to every project we undertake.
