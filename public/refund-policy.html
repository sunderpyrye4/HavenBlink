Refund Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2023

1. Overview

At HavenBlink, we strive to provide exceptional home décor consultancy services that meet and exceed our clients' expectations. Due to the custom nature of our services, refunds are generally not provided once work has commenced. However, we have established this Refund Policy to address specific circumstances and ensure fair treatment for all clients.

Please read this policy carefully before engaging our services. By making a payment, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Refund Policy.

2. General Refund Guidelines

Our refund policy varies depending on the type of service and the stage of the project. The following general guidelines apply:

  • Consultation Fees: Non-refundable once the consultation has been conducted
  • Project Deposits: Non-refundable except under specific circumstances outlined below
  • Milestone Payments: Non-refundable once the corresponding work has been delivered and approved
  • Final Payments: Non-refundable once the project is complete and deliverables have been accepted

3. Consultation Services

3.1 Initial Consultations

Initial consultation fees are charged to schedule and conduct our first meeting with you. These fees are:

  • Refundable: If cancelled at least 48 hours before the scheduled consultation
  • Non-refundable: If cancelled less than 48 hours before the consultation or if you fail to attend
  • Non-refundable: Once the consultation has been conducted

3.2 Rescheduling Consultations

You may reschedule your consultation at no additional charge if you provide at least 24 hours notice. Rescheduling with less than 24 hours notice may result in a rescheduling fee.

4. Project Deposits

Project deposits are required to secure our services and schedule your project. Deposits are typically 25-50% of the total project cost, depending on the scope of work.

Deposits are non-refundable except in the following circumstances:

  • Our Inability to Perform: If we are unable to commence work within 30 days of the agreed start date for reasons within our control
  • Early Cancellation: If you cancel within 48 hours of paying the deposit and before any work has begun
  • Mutual Agreement: If we mutually agree that we cannot meet your project requirements
  • Breach of Contract: If we materially breach our contract with you

5. Milestone Payments

For larger projects, payments are typically structured around project milestones. These payments are:

  • Due: Upon completion and approval of each milestone
  • Non-refundable: Once the milestone work has been delivered and you have approved it
  • Refundable (partially): If we fail to complete the milestone work to the agreed specifications and cannot remedy the issue within a reasonable timeframe

6. Procurement Services

For items purchased through our procurement services, the following refund policies apply:

6.1 Custom Orders

Custom-made items (including custom furniture, window treatments, and specialty items) are:

  • Non-refundable and non-returnable once ordered
  • Subject to manufacturer lead times and production schedules
  • May require additional deposits to manufacturers

6.2 Standard Items

Standard, in-stock items may be eligible for return subject to:

  • Manufacturer's return policy
  • Return within specified timeframe (typically 14-30 days)
  • Items being in original condition with all packaging
  • Restocking fees (typically 15-25% of item cost)
  • Return shipping costs being borne by the client

6.3 Damaged or Defective Items

If items arrive damaged or defective, we will:

  • Coordinate with the supplier for replacement or repair
  • Handle all claims and logistics
  • Ensure you receive a satisfactory resolution

Please inspect all deliveries immediately and report any issues within 48 hours of receipt.

7. Satisfaction Guarantee

We offer a satisfaction guarantee for our design services. If you are not satisfied with our work, we will:

  1. Listen to your concerns and understand the issues
  2. Make reasonable efforts to address your concerns
  3. Revise the design at no additional cost (up to two revisions)
  4. If still unsatisfied, discuss partial refund options based on work completed

This guarantee applies only to design services, not to purchased items or third-party services.

8. Refund Process

Step 1: Refund Request

Submit a written refund request to billing@havenblink.com with:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Project reference number
  • Detailed reason for refund request
  • Supporting documentation (if applicable)

Step 2: Review Period

We will review your request within 7-10 business days. During this period, we may:

  • Request additional information
  • Schedule a call to discuss the issues
  • Review project documentation and communications

Step 3: Decision and Resolution

We will notify you of our decision and, if approved, process the refund within 10 business days. Refunds are issued via the original payment method.

9. Refund Timeframes

Approved refunds are processed within the following timeframes:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-10 business days to appear on your statement
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • Check: 7-14 business days for mailing and processing

International refunds may take additional time due to banking procedures.

10. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable under any circumstances:

  • Consultation fees after the consultation has occurred
  • Custom design work that has been delivered and approved
  • Custom-ordered items that have entered production
  • Services that have been rendered and accepted
  • Digital products and downloads
  • Gift certificates and promotional credits

11. Dispute Resolution

If you are dissatisfied with our refund decision, you may:

  1. Request a secondary review by our management team
  2. Submit additional documentation or evidence
  3. Request mediation through a third-party dispute resolution service

We are committed to resolving all disputes fairly and amicably.

12. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.
